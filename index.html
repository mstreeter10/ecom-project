About

At Thrifty, we offer a wide range of products that are donated by members of the Rochester Community. Our products include clothes, school supplies, electronics, furniture, small appliances, books, and more. Thrifty welcomes Brockport Students to shop at a discounted rate for textbooks and other school supplies! We have a selection of used textbooks for college courses at Brockport.
